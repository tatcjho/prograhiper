Steaming rice with a spicy Asian sauce is common street fare and we akk accceot its limitations in exchange for the convenience ofa quick bite on the run. With Curbside Thai you dont't have to compromise. Building on his experience as a master chef at several fine establishments on the East Coast, Sajja Adulet has brought the taste of fine dining to the Food Cart Wars and we couldn't be happier.
